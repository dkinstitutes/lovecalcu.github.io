What is Love?

Love is a complex concept that can have different meanings depending on the context. Generally, it refers to a deep affection for another person, whether it is based on maternal instincts, sexual attraction, admiration, or even extends to inanimate objects or food. Love can vary across cultures and countries, making it challenging to find a "universal" definition. It is often divided into two categories: impersonal and interpersonal love. Impersonal love pertains to objects, principles, or goals that people may value deeply, such as life itself, a stuffed animal, or a cause. Interpersonal love, on the other hand, describes the affection between human beings, whether it is between family members, friends, or romantic partners. Throughout history, many have tried to explain love in biological, psychological, and even evolutionary terms, yet ultimately, the definition of love is subjective and varies from person to person. The results of any online love calculator or predictor should not dictate whether or not someone decides to pursue love.
